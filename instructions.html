How to Use BirdLeaf

This tool helps you notice what matters to you.

Values can shift depending on what you're focusing on or working toward. You might care about different things when you're thinking about relationships, work, leadership, or life overall.

There is no single correct set of values. Answer based on what feels important right now. Go with your first, honest response.

You will have the opportunity to revisit and change your answers every 50 words if you wish to reflect further.

Your progress autosaves on this device.
