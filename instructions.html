How to Use BirdLeaf

This tool helps you notice what matters to you.

You'll see one word at a time. For each word, click Keep if it means something to you, or Delete if it doesn't. At the end, you'll see your top values. Use Previous if you want to change your last answer.

Values can shift depending on what you're working toward.

You can set a Context - a focus area like Work, Relationships, Life, or anything you name. Context keeps you grounded in one angle as you move through the words. You can change or clear it at any point during the session.

There is no single correct set of values. Answer based on what feels important right now. Go with your first, honest response.

Your progress autosaves on this device.
